Follow your Yoga Teacher

Yoga Teachers move from studio to studio 24/7. YTYS let’s you see where you teacher teaches next regardless of the studio.

Get Hours

The Hours on your profile keeps tracks of the number of classes Yoga Student have attended and the number of Students that have practiced with a Yoga Teacher. Get ranked within YTYS.
